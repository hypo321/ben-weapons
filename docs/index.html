The Three Weapons

⚔

Fire Blitz Sword

An iron sword imbued with blaze energy. Right-click to launch a powerful fireball in the direction you're looking.

🔥 Launches fireball ⏳ 40s cooldown 🛡 Fire Resistance (passive)
⚡

Lightning Axe

A gold-tipped iron axe crackling with storm energy. Right-click to call down a lightning bolt on your target and reward yourself with rapid regeneration.

⚡ Strikes lightning ⏳ 40s cooldown 💚 Regen II for 20s (on use)
💨

Dash Mace

A mace bound with ender energy. Right-click to launch yourself ~20 blocks forward at high speed — perfect for escaping danger or closing the gap.

💨 Dashes ~20 blocks ⏳ 30s cooldown 🏃 Speed II (passive)

Installation

1

Download the plugin

Grab the latest BenWeapons.jar from the Releases page.

2

Upload to your server

Drop BenWeapons.jar into your server's plugins/ folder via FTP, SFTP, or your hosting panel's file manager.

3

Restart the server

A full restart (not reload) is recommended. You'll see a confirmation in the console when the plugin loads.

4

Craft away!

All three weapons appear in the in-game recipe book automatically. No additional configuration required.

Requirements: Paper 1.21.4+  ·  Java 21+

Admin Command

Give a weapon directly to any online player. Requires OP (or the benweapons.give permission).

/benweapons <sword|axe|mace> [player]

Omit [player] to give the weapon to yourself. Examples: /benweapons sword · /benweapons axe Steve
